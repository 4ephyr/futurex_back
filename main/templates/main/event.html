How AI is Changing the Future of Work

AI is not only automating millions of jobs, but also creating millions more. Learn how AI can help workers reskill, upskill, and find new opportunities in the changing labor market.

06 August 2023

Artificial intelligence (AI) is one of the most transformative technologies of our time. It has the potential to revolutionize various sectors of the economy, from health care to manufacturing, from education to entertainment. AI can perform tasks that require human intelligence, such as understanding natural language, recognizing images, and making decisions. AI can also learn from data and improve its performance over time.

However, AI also poses significant challenges and opportunities for the future of work. How will AI affect the demand and supply of labor? What skills will workers need to thrive in the age of AI? How will AI impact the quality and security of work? These are some of the questions that policymakers, employers, educators, and workers need to address in order to prepare for the future of work in a dynamic and uncertain environment.
